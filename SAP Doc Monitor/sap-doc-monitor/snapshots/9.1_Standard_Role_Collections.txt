The following sections provide you with an overview of the permissions the different users have with the role collections. It also provides an overview of the included roles and the relevant application identifiers.

Permissions

The majority of the roles that are included in this role collection give you the permission to view and access applications on the SAP Fiori launchpad. For example, you can view and access the Lookup application. For multiple of these applications, you can also view the included contents. For example, you can view and access the Equipment application and view all created equipment.
