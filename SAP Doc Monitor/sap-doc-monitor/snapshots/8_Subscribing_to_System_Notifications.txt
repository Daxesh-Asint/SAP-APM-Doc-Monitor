Cloud System Subscriptions

• Planned and unplanned downtime Planned downtime is the time for scheduled maintenance and upgrade during which a system cannot be used for normal productive operations. This time is used for a variety of purposes so that a system can function optimally and reliably, such as maintenance or upgrades to new releases of SAP application components, database or operating system. Unplanned downtime is the time during which a system cannot be used for normal productive operations due to unforeseen failure in hardware or software components, or operator mistakes.

What's New Announcements in Help Portal

Stay up-to-date on day-one impact changes by receiving e-mail notifications of What's New
Viewer content. This is the official channel through which you can obtain the latest
information. Hence, it is important that you subscribe to the What's New viewer.
Click on Subscribe in the What's New viewer , and select whether you want to
receive notifications for all updates or according to the filters you have
chosen..

The e-mail notification includes a link, which will open the What's New Viewer with the new or
changed items highlighted.
